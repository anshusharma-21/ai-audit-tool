# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

## Target User
- **Job Title:** Engineering Managers and Founders of Seed to Series A startups (5-50 employees).
- **Pain Point:** Managing fragmented AI invoices (Cursor, OpenAI, Claude) across personal cards and corporate accounts.

## Distribution Channels
- **X (Twitter):** Sharing "SaaS Leakage" threads and tagging startup founders.
- **Subreddits:** r/startups and r/SaaS (Providing value-first audits).
- **The Unfair Channel:** Cold-emailing companies with high developer headcounts on LinkedIn, offering a "Zero-Cost Audit."

## The First 100 Users
- Day 1-10: Manual outreach on LinkedIn/X to 20 founders/day.
- Day 11-20: Posting the tool on Product Hunt and Indie Hackers.
- Day 21-30: Partnering with startup incubators to offer the tool as a "Member Perk."

## Week 1 Success Metrics
- 50+ unique audits completed.
- 5+ "High Savings" consultations booked via the tool.
- 20% conversion rate from Landing Page to Audit completion.
