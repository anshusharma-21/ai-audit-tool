# Project Economics

## Revenue Model
- **Direct Savings:** 30% flat reduction in AI subscription costs for enterprises.
- **Efficiency Gain:** Consolidation of multiple bills into a single Credex invoice saves ~5 hours of accounting work per month.

## Cost to Value Ratio
- **User Spend:** $25/seat (Standard)
- **Credex Price:** $17/seat
- **Net Annual Value:** $96 savings per active seat.

## Scaling to $1M ARR (The Math)
To hit $1M ARR in 18 months:
- **Target:** ~10,000 managed AI seats via Credex.
- **Strategy:** If each seat generates a $10 fee/margin for Credex, we need 10,000 seats. 
- **Acquisition:** At a 5% conversion rate from Audit -> Purchase, we need 200,000 users to run the audit.
- **CAC:** If our GTM (X threads & Reddit) is organic, CAC will be near $0. If we use paid ads at $2/click, we need a high conversion rate to stay profitable.

## Unit Economics
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** Average startup stays for 24 months = ~$200 profit per seat.
- **Payback Period:** Less than 1 month due to the low overhead of the digital credit pool.
